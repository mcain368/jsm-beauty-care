Meet Millicent
Owner of JSM Beauty Care

For over three decades, I've dedicated my hands and heart to making every woman, man, and child who sits in my chair feel like royalty. Hair isn't just hair to me it's expression, it's culture, it's confidence.

Growing up in Jamaica taught me that beauty comes in infinite forms. Whether you're looking for protective styles that last, a flawless wig install, or to embrace your natural texture, I'm here to bring your vision to life.

At JSM Beauty Care, you're not just a client—you're family.
